Rick and Morty-Verse

This is an app that gives you information on the famous rick and morty show. You can look at different characters throughout the show and get a bit of information on them. The app also lists the episodes of the show in order from the first season. The different locations where the various episodes of the show take place are also in the app. The user has the option of voting for their favorite character, episode and location from the show.

Features

  • Characters of the show and information about them
  • Seasons and Episodes
  • Locations Rick and Morty have travelled to
  • A search bar
  • A vote form to vote for your favorite character, episode and location

Technologies used

  • JavaScript: Used to create dynamic and interactive user experiences on the frontend.
  • HTML: The structure and content of the web pages.
  • CSS: Styling and layout of the web pages.
  • Public API: Used to fetch data for the site.
